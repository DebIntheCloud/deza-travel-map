THE FOOD DESTINATION DEZA

The Deza region, known for its rich Galician heritage, is a fantastic gastronomic destination, offering a variety of exciting flavors rooted in tradition. A highlight of Galician cuisine is its fresh, high-quality seafood, with locally caught fish and shellfish taking center stage. The region is also renowned for its exceptional meats, paired with the finest fresh produce, creating dishes that reflect both the land and sea.
